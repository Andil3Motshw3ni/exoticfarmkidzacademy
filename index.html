About Our Academy

Exotic Farm Kidz Academy is a playful learning center focused on early childhood development in a safe, farm-style environment. We combine creativity with play-based learning and outdoor farm experiences.

Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment where children can explore, learn, and grow through hands-on activities, caring educators, and meaningful interactions with nature and animals.

  • Caring and experienced educators
  • Safe, secure farm-style environment
  • Play-based learning approach
  • Outdoor farm experiences
  • Early childhood development focus
  • Creative and sensory learning
